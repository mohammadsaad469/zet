Art Exibits

Each year Jourdean S. Lorah participates in many fine art exhibits at The Rehoboth Art League in various categories. Those various categories include; Contour Pen & Ink drawings, water color compositions, pastel compositions, photography, jewelry and craft. The following lists of art exhibits is presently current in the last five years. In addition to the work that is displayed in the Member's Gallery at the Rehoboth Art League is a slide show that can be viewed at The Delaware Council for the Arts in Wilmington, Delaware.

February 2015
Presently, I am listed as an artist online at The Rehoboth Art League in Rehoboth Beach Delaware . An updated artist statement has been completed with an image of my work. All up coming exhibits will be included in the Member's Gallery which will display many of my sterling silver pieces. A variety of work will be displayed throughout the year. I also participate in other exhibits such as the Fine Art exhibits and the Craft exhibits.

February 2014
The Member's Art Gallery of Rehoboth Art League in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. The exhibits in the Member's Gallery displayed many of my finely crafted pieces of sterling silver jewelry and cabochons. The shadow box exhibit in July 2013 featured hand crafted shadow boxes made out of wood with photography. Each box is superimposed with photos on the inside of the box representing a particular theme.

February 2013
The Member's Art Gallery of Rehoboth Art League in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. Presently, The Member's Gallery has sold and displayed many of my hand crafted pieces of sterling silver jewelry. Pen & ink contour drawings are also on display in the artist rack.

February 2012
The Member's Art Gallery at the Rehoboth Art League in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. Each exhibit included, sterling silver jewelry finely crafted with freshwater pearls, turquoise, coral, and shell.

February 2011
The Member's Art Gallery of Rehoboth Art League in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. The exhibits in the Member's Gallery displayed many of my finely crafted pieces of sterling silver jewelry and cabochons. The shadow box exhibit in July 2013 featured hand crafted shadow boxes made out of wood with photography. Each box is superimposed with photos on the inside of the box representing a particular theme.

February 2010
The Member's Gallery Exhibit included several exhibits in the following categories; The Fine Art Exhibit, The Craft Exhibit, and Photography.
